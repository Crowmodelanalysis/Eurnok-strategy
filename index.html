Mechanism & Insight

The core mechanism

Higher oil should strengthen NOK — but currency markets are non-linear. When U.S. equities sell off, Norwegian sovereign wealth fund hedging of foreign equity holdings dominates. NOK selling by equity hedgers overwhelms the oil-positive story entirely.

Why single-variable correlation fails

Overlaying oil against EURNOK misses the interaction. The oil/EURNOK relationship is regime-dependent: bullish only when equities are stable or rising. Correlation grids are a starting point, not an answer.
